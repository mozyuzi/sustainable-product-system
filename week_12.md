# Week 12 – Design Reflection and Technical Evaluation

## Overview

The objective of Week 12 was to evaluate the final design of the system and reflect on the decisions made throughout development.

## Changes

During this week we reviewed the architecture, design principles, testing approach, and use of design patterns.

The following areas were evaluated:

- Layered Architecture
- Strategy Pattern
- Single Responsibility Principle (SRP)
- Open/Closed Principle (OCP)
- Dependency Inversion Principle (DIP)
- Testability and maintainability

We also identified possible future improvements, such as stronger input validation, persistent storage, and expanded test coverage.

## Result

The evaluation confirmed that the system follows a clear layered architecture and uses the Strategy pattern to support multiple environmental impact calculation methods. The project meets the requirements while remaining maintainable, testable, and extensible.
