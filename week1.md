# Week 1 – Requirements and Initial Analysis

---

## Overview

This week focuses on understanding the problem domain and translating the project description into structured requirements. The goal is to clearly define what the system should do and establish a foundation for the design in later weeks.

---

## Functional Requirements

- The system must allow users to create and manage products  
- The system must store materials associated with products  
- The system must support products composed of multiple materials  
- The system must calculate environmental impact for a product  
- The system must provide recycling guidance for products  
- The system must classify products into categories  

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- The system must be maintainable and easy to modify  
- The system must be extensible to support new impact calculation strategies  
- The system must be testable  
- The system must have clear separation of concerns  

---

## System Boundary

### Inside the system:
- Product management  
- Material management  
- Environmental impact calculation  
- Recycling guidance generation  

### Outside the system:
- User interface (console or GUI)  
- Database or file storage  
- External services  

---

## Domain Concepts

- Product  
- Material  
- Category  
- Lifespan  
- RecyclingCategory  
- RecyclingGuidance  
- ImpactCalculationStrategy
