# Week 10 – Sequence Diagram

## Overview

The objective of Week 10 was to document the behaviour of the system using a UML Sequence Diagram.

## Changes

We created a sequence diagram that describes the **Create Product** use case. The diagram follows the flow from the user interacting with the console interface, through the application layer, and into the domain layer.

The sequence diagram includes:

- User
- ConsoleUI
- ProductService
- Product
- Material

The diagram reflects the actual implementation of the system and demonstrates how responsibilities are distributed across the layers.

## Result

Creating the sequence diagram helped us verify that our layered architecture was correctly implemented and that the flow of information between objects matched our design.
